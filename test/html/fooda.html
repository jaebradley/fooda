MexiCali Burrito Co.
MexiCali Burrito Co. is a family owned taqueria in the style of the San Fransisco Mission District. At MexiCali you'll find that our passion for high quality, fresh, healthy ingredients is reflected in the distinct flavors of our recipes. These recipes were used by the first burrito pushcart in New York City and over time have grown to include health conscious additions like whole wheat tortillas, non-fat yogurt and vegan friendly soups, salads and chili.
